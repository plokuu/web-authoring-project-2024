A little about myself...

My name's Johnathan O'Reilly and I've been a
vintage car collector for the better part of 35 years now.

My love for cars flourished from my early years.
My father, Joesoph O'Reilly, was a prolific formula
one racer in the 50s to 60s.
Dad always let me help him work on his cars and I gained great appreciation in his craft.
Tragically, his life was cut short in a terrible collision in the Grand Prix.

When I was 30, I opened my very own auto-repair shop down at Sandymount and I took on repairing
and rejuvenating vintage vehicles from his time.
Since, We've franchised the business to become a nationwide sucesss.
